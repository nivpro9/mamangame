HOW TO PLAY

✋ Hold the screen to fly up — release to fall down
🟩 Fly through the gaps in the pillars — don't crash!
📦 Collect ammo boxes to charge your cannon (buy Cannon in Upgrades)
🪙 Grab coins to spend on vehicles and upgrades
🛬 Reach the distance goal — then fly onto the runway to land!
